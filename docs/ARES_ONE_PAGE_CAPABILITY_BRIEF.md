## Positioning

ARES is a governed autonomous engineering runtime that validates proposed work, gates execution, records operator decisions, and produces tamper-evident evidence receipts.

## Problem

AI engineering agents can act faster than teams can verify. Buyers need proof of what was proposed, approved, blocked, and executed.

## What ARES Currently Demonstrates

- Validation / Reality Pass
- MRVL validation gate
- Operator Approval Ledger
- Execution Approval Gate policy
- `EvidenceReceipt` / `EvidenceLedger`
- One-command client demo verification runner

## Proof Points

- Freeze tags for each governance lane.
- Runtime probes for validation gates, approval receipts, and execution approval policy.
- Isolated ledger tests for evidence-writing paths.
- Production evidence asserted unchanged in probes.
- SHA-256 hash-chained evidence ledger.

Australian SMEs, platform teams, CTOs, security/compliance-sensitive engineering teams, and AI governance leads.

## Current Non-Goals

- Not claiming full autonomous production readiness.
- Not claiming regulatory certification.
- Not claiming zero liability.
- Not replacing legal/compliance review.
- Not hardwiring licensing automation yet.
